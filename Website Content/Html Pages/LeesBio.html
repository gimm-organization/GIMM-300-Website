Lee Rasmussen

 

Hello! My name is Lee Rasmussen! I’m currently a GIMM student at boise state university. This is my second year in the GIMM program and I have gained knowledge in design, programming in several languages, basic hardware implementation, video software, and more. In GIMM 300 I hope to learn the best ways to connect to people through technology, and learn the techniques that will help me establish these connections. I’m interested in eventually entering the field of software development, in whatever capacity that may be. I think there is a lot of responsibility to be learned from technology development of any kind.

Technology is such an integral part of modern society, and people often focus on the next best thing without stopping to think about the moral implications. I think that developers have the opportunity to create things that can change the world, but the also have the responsibility to not create something that harms it.
